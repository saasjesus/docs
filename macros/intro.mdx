## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Inbox">
    Go to your [Inbox](https://armin.cx/app/_/inbox) and click on any random Ticket
  </Step>
  <Step title="How to create">
    Then click on the "Macro" Button on the bottom of the Reply-Box

    ![images/macro_btn.png](/images/macro_btn.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your first Macro">
    Next you can click the "\+" Icon to create your first Macro.

    1. Give it a Name
    2. Enter the content & use "@" to see the available Variables you can use in your Macros
  </Step>
</Steps>
