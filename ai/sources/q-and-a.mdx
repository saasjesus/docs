### `Main Benefits`

- Perfect Training Data for repetetive questions which are not customer-specific

### `Where to find?`

- Under [AI](https://armin.cx/app/_/ai/agents) -\> "**Your AI**" -\> **Sources**-\> **Q&A**

---

Q&A is good training data for the same repetetive Questions\
Good for when users have **clear, answerable questions**.

### Examples

- **"How long does shipping take to Germany?"**
- **"Can I return a worn item?"**
- **"What size is Medium in EU sizing?"**
- **"Do you ship internationally?"**
- **"Is the promo code still valid?"**
