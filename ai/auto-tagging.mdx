### `Main Benefits`

- Tagging Tickets for better filtering in [Views](/inbox/views)

### `Where to find?`

- Under [AI](https://armin.cx/app/_/ai) -\> "**Your AI**" -\> **General**

---

You can simply create your Tags & together with a short description on "when to Tag" this Tag, the AI takes over and automatically tags tickets when they are `CREATED`

By default, we create those **6 Tags**, which are automatically activated:

- Retoure, Reklamation
- Stornierung
- Produkte
- Bestellung
- Zahlung
- Lieferung, Versand
